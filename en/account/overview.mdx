## Step 2: Create an Account

Click on the **Create an account** link at the bottom of the login form.  
You will be redirected to the registration page.

![Step 2 - Create Account Button](/assets/images/account/step2-creer-compte.png)

---

## Step 3: Fill in Your Personal Information

Complete the form with your details:  
- **First name**  
- **Last name**  
- **Email address**  
- **Phone number**  
- **Password**  
- **Confirm password**

Once the form is submitted, your account will be created âœ….  
You will automatically be redirected to the **login page**.

![Step 3 - Registration Form](/assets/images/account/step3-formulaire.png)

---

## Step 4: Log in to Your Account

Enter your credentials:  
- **Email address**  
- **Password**

Then click **Login**.

![Step 4 - Account Login](/assets/images/account/step4-connexion.png)

---

## Step 5: Create an Organization and an Account (Store)

On your first login, you will be asked to create an **organization** and an **account (or store)** in a single form.  

Provide the following details:  
- **Organization name**  
- **Description** (what your organization is about)  
- **Account (or store) name** (e.g., Yabetoo Congo Store)  
- **Location** (*currently available only for Congo*)  

![Step 5 - Create Organization and Account](/assets/images/account/step5-organisation.png)
