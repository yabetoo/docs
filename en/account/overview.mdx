You can start using your Yabetoo account in test environments as soon as it is created. In a sandbox environment, you can simulate the use of all Yabetoo features without moving any real money. Once your account is activated, you can accept payments, create additional accounts, and manage a team.
if you haven't created an account yet, [create a Yabetoo account](https://app.yabetoo.com/register)
