## What is Yabetoo?

Yabetoo is a comprehensive payment platform designed to simplify online transactions for businesses of all sizes. Built with developers in mind, Yabetoo provides secure, reliable, and easy-to-integrate payment solutions that help you focus on growing your business while we handle the complexities of payment processing.

Our platform offers multiple integration options, from simple hosted checkout pages to advanced API integrations, ensuring that you can implement payment solutions that perfectly match your business needs and technical requirements.

## Core Payment Solutions

### Hosted Checkout Pages

Experience the simplest way to accept payments with our professionally designed, secure checkout pages. Simply redirect your customers to our hosted environment where they can complete their purchases safely. This solution reduces your PCI compliance burden while providing an optimized user experience.

**Key Benefits:**

- **Zero PCI compliance burden** - We handle all sensitive payment data
- **Mobile-optimized design** - Seamless experience across all devices
- **Brand customization** - Match your brand colors and styling
- **Multi-language support** - Serve customers globally
- **Instant setup** - Start accepting payments in minutes

### Advanced API Integration

For businesses requiring more control and customization, our REST API provides flexible payment processing capabilities directly within your application. Build custom payment flows that integrate seamlessly with your existing systems.

**Features:**

- **Payment Intents** - Create and manage payment transactions programmatically
- **Real-time status updates** - Track payment progress instantly
- **Flexible confirmation flows** - Support for 3D Secure and other authentication methods
- **Comprehensive webhook system** - Receive instant notifications of payment events

## Additional Payment Services

### Disbursements

Transfer funds from your account to customer accounts with our disbursement API. Perfect for marketplaces, gig economy platforms, and any business that needs to pay out to multiple recipients.

**Capabilities:**

- **Asynchronous processing** - Efficient handling of bulk payments
- **Real-time webhooks** - Instant notifications when disbursements complete
- **Partner account exclusive** - Available for verified business accounts

### Remittance Services

Enable international money transfers with our remittance solution, designed for businesses operating across borders or serving customers with international payment needs.

### Payment Links

Create shareable payment links for easy collection of payments without requiring a full e-commerce setup. Perfect for invoicing, donations, or one-off transactions.

## Developer Experience

### SDKs and Libraries

We provide official SDKs to accelerate your integration:

- **PHP SDK** - Full-featured library for PHP applications
- **Java SDK** - Enterprise-ready integration for Java environments
- **More languages coming soon** - Including Python, Node.js, and .NET

### WordPress Plugin

For WordPress users, our official plugin provides seamless integration with WooCommerce and custom WordPress sites, enabling payment acceptance without any coding required.

### Testing Environment

Comprehensive testing tools help you validate your integration:

- **Sandbox environment** - Full API parity with production
- **Test card numbers** - Simulate various payment scenarios
- **Webhook testing** - Validate your event handling logic

## Security and Compliance

Yabetoo is built with security at its core:

- **PCI DSS Level 1 certified** - Highest level of payment security
- **Advanced fraud detection** - Machine learning-powered risk assessment
- **3D Secure support** - Enhanced authentication for card payments
- **End-to-end encryption** - All data protected in transit and at rest
