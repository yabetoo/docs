Yabetoo payment links allow you to quickly create customized payment pages that you can share with your customers via email, SMS, or any other communication channel.

## Benefits of Payment Links

- **Simplicity**: Create payment links in a few clicks, without needing technical skills.
- **Flexibility**: Customize the amount, currency, and add product or service information.
- **Easy Sharing**: Send the link via any communication channel.
- **Real-time Tracking**: Monitor payment status and receive notifications.
- **Security**: Payments are processed on secure pages hosted by Yabetoo.

## How It Works

1. **Creation**: Create a payment link by specifying the amount, currency, and product details.
2. **Sharing**: Share the link with your customer via email, SMS, WhatsApp, or any other method.
3. **Payment**: The customer clicks the link and is redirected to a secure payment page.
4. **Confirmation**: Once payment is complete, you receive a notification and the customer receives a confirmation.

## Use Cases

- **Invoices**: Send invoices with an integrated payment link.
- **Donations**: Collect donations for your organization.
- **Product Sales**: Sell products or services without needing an online store.
- **Events**: Sell tickets for your events.
- **Services**: Bill your clients for services rendered.
